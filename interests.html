Books

My five most recent reviews, updated daily from Goodreads:

The Prize: The Epic Quest for Oil, Money, and Power
Daniel Yergin
★★★★★
I was impressed by the rigorous analysis of multi-national mega-deals. I was surprised by how many historical figures surfaced throughout the narrative. And I was shocked at how well the book's central thesis holds up: that mastery of oil was the principle strategic goal for countries in the 20th century.
Misery
Stephen King
★★★★★
Creepy, upsetting, an iconic antagonist. But what takes Misery to the next level is King battling his own demons: rich commentary on addiction, the craft of writing, and the double-edge sword of fandom. One of the best, and certainly the tightest, books from the King.
